Echoes Kitchen is an LA based pop-up serving USDA Prime, fresh-ground cheeseburgers.

Echoes Burgers - The pop up

The flavor of the meat always comes first. Tripp's burger blend is a combination of USDA Prime chuck, top sirloin and bacon. You can catch us at our Palms & Sepulveda pop-up for lunch and at various breweries around LA. Join us on Instagram for our weekly schedule and location updates.

Catering Services

You like a party. We like a party. Tripp Burgers will treat your guests to fresh-ground, all-natural Cheeseburgers featuring Tripp's unique blend of meat*. *Contains a little bit of pork
