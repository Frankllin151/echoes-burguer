Cheeseburger

Indulge in our classic Cheeseburger, featuring a juicy *burger blend patty topped with fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, tangy pickles, and melted American cheese. Get ready for a flavor explosion with our Texas Burger! Served on a brioche bun, it's a true delight for your taste buds. *Our burger blend contains pork, but we offer the option for pork-free preparation upon request.

Bacon Burger:

Satisfy your cravings with our irresistible Bacon Burger. Picture a juicy *burger blend patty layered with melted American cheese, caramelized onions, and crispy bacon. The savory flavors blend perfectly to create a burger that is rich, indulgent, and guaranteed to please. *Our burger blend contains pork, but we offer the option for pork-free preparation upon request.

Açaí Bowl:

Experience a taste of tropical bliss with our refreshing Açaí Bowl. Enjoy a delicious blend of açaí berries topped with your choice of fresh and vibrant toppings, such as bananas, strawberries, blueberries, crunchy granola, and a drizzle of sweet honey. It's a delightful treat that will transport you to paradise.

French Fries:

Crunch into our perfectly golden and crispy French Fries. Served hot and seasoned to perfection, these fries are the ideal companion to your burger, adding that extra touch of deliciousness to your meal.

Mark Tripp, a former Ad Guy of 35 years, caught George Motz’s film “Hamburger America,” on PBS one night. Inspired by the burger joints Motz featured, Tripp began experimenting with creating his own burger blend.


For Tripp the flavor of the meat always comes first. His recipe is a combination of USDA Prime cuts of top sirloin, chuck and bacon. He counts on award-winning butcher, Richard Schwartzberg, to grind each batch to his specifications.

What started as a passion project turned into a labor of love. In May, 2019 Tripp had the opportunity to share his burger with George Motz, who gave it his official stamp of approval. Tripp then competed in the LA Food and Wine Festival EPIC Burger Throwdown and has since been featured in KTLA's LAunscripted, LA Taco, and Infatuation LA.


"That's F'ing Good," was Seth Rogen's reaction when biting into a Tripp Burger during an episode of the The Burger Show hosted by Alvin Cailan with Tiffani Thiessen and Adam Richman.
